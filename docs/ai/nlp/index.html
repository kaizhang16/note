    自然语言处理

    LSTM 与 GRU

    请参考 https://towardsdatascience.com/illustrated-guide-to-lstms-and-gru-s-a-step-by-step-explanation-44e9eb85bf21。

    LSTM

    请参考 https://colah.github.io/posts/2015-08-Understanding-LSTMs/。

    Forget gate:

    \[ f_t = \sigma(W_f \cdot [h_{t-1}, x_t] + b_f) \qquad(1)\]

    Input gate:

    \[ i_t = \sigma(W_i \cdot [h_{t-1}, x_t] + b_i) \qquad(2)\]

    Cell state:

    \[ \begin{align} \tilde{C}_t &= \tanh(W_C \cdot [h_{t-1}, x_t] + b_C) \\ C_t &= f_t \odot C_{t-1} + i_t \odot \tilde{C}_t \end{align} \qquad(3)\]

    Output:

    \[ \begin{align} o_t &= \sigma(W_o \cdot [h_{t-1}, x_t] + b_o) \\ h_t &= o_t \odot \tanh(C_t) \end{align} \qquad(4)\]

    GRU[1]

    Reset Gate:

    \[r_t = \sigma(W_r \cdot [h_{t-1}, x_t])\qquad(5)\]

    Update Gate:

    \[z_t = \sigma(W_z \cdot [h_{t-1}, x_t])\qquad(6)\]

    Output:

    \[ \begin{align} \tilde{h}_t &= \tanh(W_h \cdot [r_t \odot h_{t-1}, x_t]) \\ h_t &= (1-z_t) \odot h_{t_1} + z_t \odot \tilde{h}_t \end{align} \qquad(7)\]

    参考文献

    [1] CHO K, VAN MERRIËNBOER B, GULCEHRE C, 等. Learning phrase representations using RNN encoder-decoder for statistical machine translation[J]. arXiv preprint arXiv:1406.1078, 2014.
